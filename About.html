Our History

Xavier "Avi" Bojorquez is the founder and owner of Avi's Confecitons. For him, it started out as a side gig with his fast food job. His clients were usually his friends, bosses, and family. Starting off with selling cakepops and cupcakes, he made everything in his home kitchen with recipies he adjusts and tests to create new flavors. Plans of a cafe have long been in Xavier's head bust didn't know where to start.
